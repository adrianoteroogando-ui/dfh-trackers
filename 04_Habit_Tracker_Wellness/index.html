How to Use Your Habit Tracker
1

Add Your Habits

Go to "My Habits" and add the habits you want to track. Start with 3-5 habits maximum for best results.

2

Check Off Daily

Every day, go to "Daily Habits", check off what you completed, and tap Save Day.

3

Track Your Streak

The Dashboard shows your current streak and monthly completion rate automatically.

4

Review History

The History tab shows your daily scores over time so you can spot patterns and improve.

5

Your Data is Safe

All data is saved locally on your device. Private, secure, no account needed.
