How to Use Your Sleep & Recovery Log
1

Set Your Goals

Go to the Goals tab and enter your target sleep hours, bedtime, wake-up time, and quality/energy targets.

2

Log Every Morning

Each morning, go to Sleep Log and record the previous night's bedtime, wake-up time, quality rating (1–10), and energy level when you woke up.

3

Track Recovery

Use the Recovery tab to log workout intensity, muscle soreness, resting heart rate, and water intake. This shows how training affects your sleep.

4

Check Your Dashboard

The Dashboard calculates your average sleep hours, quality score, and energy level automatically. Use it to spot trends and make adjustments.

5

Your Data is Safe

All data is saved locally on your device using your browser's storage. It stays completely private and never leaves your computer.
