How to Use Your 12-Week Challenge
1

Set Up Your Challenge

Go to Goal Setting. Enter your start date, starting weight, goal weight, and how many workouts per week you'll do.

2

Log Each Week

At the end of every week, go to Week Log and record your workouts, weight, calories, and how you felt.

3

Track Nutrition Daily

Use the Nutrition Log to track daily calories, protein, carbs and fat for maximum results.

4

Watch Your Progress

The Dashboard shows your 12-week grid filling up as you complete weeks. Satisfying to watch it grow!
