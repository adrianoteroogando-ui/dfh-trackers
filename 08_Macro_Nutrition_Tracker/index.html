How to Use Your Macro Tracker
1

Calculate Your TDEE

Go to TDEE Calculator. Enter your age, gender, weight, height and activity level to get your calorie targets.

2

Set Macro Goals

Go to Macro Goals and enter your daily calorie, protein, carb and fat targets.

3

Log Every Meal

After each meal, go to Daily Log and enter the food with its macros. Be as accurate as possible.

4

Check Your Dashboard

The Dashboard shows today's progress bars for calories, protein, carbs and fat in real-time.
