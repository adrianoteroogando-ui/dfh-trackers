How to Use Your Tracker
1

Set Your Goals

Go to the Goals tab and enter your starting weight, goal weight, daily calorie target, and target date. This activates your progress tracking.

2

Log Daily

Every day, go to Daily Log and enter your weight, calories eaten, calories burned, water intake and notes. The more consistent you are, the better your dashboard.

3

Track Measurements

Every 2-4 weeks, go to Measurements and log your body measurements. This shows your body composition changes beyond just weight.

4

Check Your Dashboard

The Dashboard updates automatically with every entry. Check it daily to stay motivated and on track with your transformation.

5

Your Data is Safe

All your data is saved locally on your device using your browser's storage. It stays private — never leaves your computer. To back up, use your browser's bookmark or save the file to a folder you back up.
