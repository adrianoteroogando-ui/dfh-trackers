How to Use Your Workout Tracker
1

Log Every Set

Go to Workout Log and log each set as you go in the gym. Enter exercise, sets, reps, and weight.

2

Track Progressive Overload

Each session, try to beat your last weight or reps. Your PRs tab shows your best lifts automatically.

3

Check Your PRs

The Personal Records tab auto-calculates your heaviest lift for every exercise — no manual tracking needed.

4

Use the Exercise Library

The library has 20 exercises with form cues. Use it as a reference before or during your workout.

5

Your Data is Safe

All data is saved locally on your device. It never leaves your computer and stays completely private.
