How to Use Your Weight Loss Tracker
1

Set Your Goals

Go to Goals tab. Enter your starting weight, goal weight, daily calorie target and target date.

2

Log Your Food Daily

Use the Food Log tab to track every meal. Log breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks with calories and protein.

3

Weigh In Weekly

Go to Weigh-In tab every week (same day, same time, same conditions) and log your weight.

4

Check Your Dashboard

The Dashboard shows your current weight, total lost, today's calories and overall progress automatically.

5

Your Data is Safe

All data is saved locally on your device. It never leaves your computer and stays completely private.
