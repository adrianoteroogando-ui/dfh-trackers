How to Use Your Meal Planner
1

Plan Your Week

Go to Meal Planner and add meals for each day. Plan all 3 meals + snacks for the entire week on Sunday.

2

Build Your Grocery List

Add all ingredients you need to the Grocery List. Check items off as you shop.

3

Log What You Eat

Each day, use the Food Log to record what you actually ate with calories and protein.

4

Review Your Dashboard

The Dashboard shows your weekly plan overview, today's calories, and grocery list status.
